Platform

Everything your team needs,
nothing they don't

FieldOS is purpose-built for door-to-door field sales — not a generic CRM with a maps plugin bolted on.

🗺️
GPS Territory Mapping
Upload KML polygons to define exact coverage zones. Reps see only their territory. Cluster markers keep the map clean at any zoom level.
👆
One-Tap Dispositions
Not Home, Sold, No Interest, In Contract, Go Back Later — log a door outcome in under two seconds. No typing, no friction, no dropped data.
📊
Manager Dashboard
Live rep locations, sale counts, time-of-day charts, leaderboards, and territory coverage heatmaps. Know exactly what's happening in the field right now.
📅
Install Scheduling
Built-in slot picker that reads from your Google Sheet calendar. Reps book installs on the doorstep. No phone tag. No double-bookings.
🤖
AI Territory Intelligence
Claude analyzes your coverage data and surfaces actionable recommendations — which blocks to revisit, what time of day converts best, which reps need coaching.

Multi-Tenant & Isolated
Every company gets a fully isolated workspace. Your data never touches another customer's. Role-based access, audit logs, and SOC 2-ready architecture.

FAQ

Common questions

Does it work without a cell signal?
+
Yes. FieldOS is a Progressive Web App with offline support. Reps can log dispositions and view their address list without signal. Data syncs automatically when connection resumes. KML territory overlays are cached on device.
Do reps need to download anything from the App Store?
+
No. Reps open their invite link in a browser and add the app to their home screen. It looks and feels like a native app, works on iOS and Android, and your IT department doesn't need to manage app distribution.
Where is my data stored?
+
Each workspace gets its own isolated Google Sheet as the backend store — which means you can always open it directly in Google Sheets if you want a raw data export. Nothing is commingled with other customers. You own your data entirely.
Can I upload our own territory boundaries?
+
Yes. Upload a KML file exported from Google My Maps or any GIS tool and FieldOS draws your territory polygons on the map. You can also drop pins manually or draw zones directly on the map. Multiple territories are supported on Growth and Enterprise plans.
How does the 14-day trial work?
+
You get full access to all features of your chosen plan for 14 days. No credit card required to start. You'll receive a reminder 3 days before the trial ends. If you decide not to continue, your data stays in your Google Sheet — nothing is deleted.
Can I white-label this for our company?
+
Yes, on the Enterprise plan. We replace the FieldOS logo with yours, customize the color scheme to match your brand, and update the app name. Your reps see your company's branding throughout. Contact us to discuss.
Does it integrate with Salesforce or HubSpot?
+
CRM integration is available on the Enterprise plan via webhook. Every sale automatically pushes a record to your CRM. We also support Zapier for connecting to 6,000+ other apps. Self-service integration setup is on our roadmap for Growth plan.
