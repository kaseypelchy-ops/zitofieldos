Privacy Policy

📅 Effective: January 1, 2026 🔄 Last updated: March 7, 2026 📍 Applies to: fieldos.io and all FieldOS services
This Privacy Policy explains how FieldOS ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our field sales platform, including our web application, admin dashboard, and related services. By using FieldOS, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.
01

Overview

FieldOS is a software-as-a-service platform built for door-to-door field sales teams. We provide territory mapping, door disposition tracking, sales logging, schedule management, and team analytics tools to businesses ("Workspace Administrators") and their employees or contractors ("Reps").

FieldOS operates on a workspace isolation model. Each customer company ("workspace") has its own dedicated Google Sheet as a data store. Your data is not commingled with data from other companies. We do not sell your data, use your sales data for advertising, or share it with other FieldOS customers.

Key principle: Your company's field sales data belongs to you. We are a processor of that data on your behalf, not the data controller who owns it. You can export or delete your data at any time.
02

Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways: information you provide directly, information collected automatically as you use the service, and information from third-party services you connect.

Information you provide directly:

  • Workspace setup: company name, industry vertical, team size
  • Account information: name, work email address, phone number, job title
  • Billing information: collected and processed exclusively by Stripe; FieldOS never stores full credit card numbers
  • Territory data: geographic boundaries, street addresses, KML files you upload
  • Field activity: door dispositions, sale records, schedule bookings, chat messages
  • Customer information: names, phone numbers, and addresses of your customers that you enter when logging sales

Information collected automatically:

  • GPS location data when Reps use the field app (only while the app is in active use; not background-tracked)
  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • IP address and general geographic region
  • Usage patterns: which features are used, session duration, error logs
  • Timestamps on all actions (when dispositions are logged, when sales are recorded)

Information from third-party services:

  • Google: when you connect a Google account for calendar or scheduling features, we receive basic profile information (name, email) and access to the specific Google Sheet created for your workspace
  • Stripe: payment status, subscription tier, and billing history (never raw card data)

04

Data Storage

FieldOS uses Google Sheets as a primary data store. When a new workspace is created, we automatically generate a dedicated Google Sheet in our service account. This sheet contains all of your workspace's data: rep accounts, addresses, dispositions, sales records, schedules, and configuration.

You own your Google Sheet. Workspace Administrators can request a transfer of ownership of their workspace Sheet at any time, giving you direct access to all your data in a format you already know how to work with — no data export required.

All Google Sheets data is stored in Google's infrastructure, which is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and their enterprise data processing agreements. Google does not use your Sheet data for advertising.

Additional operational data (workspace registry, subscription status) is stored in Google infrastructure within the United States. If you are located in the European Economic Area, data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses for such transfers.

06

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within your workspace: Workspace Administrators can view all Rep activity, including GPS location (while active), dispositions logged, and sales records. Reps can only see data within their assigned territory.
  • Service providers: Third-party services listed above, solely to operate the platform.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of FieldOS, our users, or the public, we may disclose information to law enforcement or government authorities.
  • Business transfers: If FieldOS is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you by email before your data is subject to a materially different privacy policy.
  • With your explicit consent: For any other purpose, only with your express permission.
Workspace isolation: We never share one company's data with another company using FieldOS. Your competitors cannot access your territory data, rep lists, or sales records.
07

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Workspace data is isolated — each workspace has its own dedicated Google Sheet with access controls
  • Invite tokens and authentication are scoped per workspace and expire or can be regenerated by administrators
  • Stripe handles all payment data under their PCI-DSS Level 1 certification — we never process or store raw card numbers
  • Audit logs record disposition changes, login events, and administrative actions
  • Admin accounts can force-deactivate rep access at any time

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@fieldos.io.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format (CSV export)
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

Workspace Administrators can exercise most of these rights directly through the Admin Dashboard. For requests that require our involvement, contact privacy@fieldos.io. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

If you are in the European Economic Area and believe we have not addressed your concerns, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
